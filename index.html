About Us

Our History In 1949 Saraswati Education Society was founded by Late Mr.P.H.Sidhwa and his wife Late Mrs.T.P.Sidhwa. They took over “Zorastrian Boarding High School “(ZBHS) pioneered by Mr. Jal Master between 1946-47 which was in great financial trouble. They started the school with 40 students and that was the first step towards the creation of a new society. It was registered as a public charitable trust and had appointed qualified staff and competent Headmaster Late Mr. Santaram Varma and immediately within 2 to 3 years, Govt. gave grant-in-aid to the school and it is because of the self service and the sincere efforts of each and every member of this school, that it has risen to such a high pedestal and therefore day-by-day the strength of the students is also increasing at steep rate. Presently the school is running in two shifts i.e. English Medium in the morning shift 1. English Prep. School from Standard – K.G. to 7’th (Since 1965). 2. Deolali High School from Standard – 8’th to 10’th (Since 1970). Marathi Medium in the afternoon shift 1. Anganwadi — Standard K.G. only (Since 1986). 2. Saraswati Vidya Mandir from standard 1’st to 4’th (Since 1986). 3. Deolali High School from std. 5’th to 10’th (Since 1968). During its earlier days Saraswati Education Society primarily educated only about 40 students but today it caters to the needs of 3500 students and diffuses knowledge among the people of Deolali Camp and its surrounding villages.
